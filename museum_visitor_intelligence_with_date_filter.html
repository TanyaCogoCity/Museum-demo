Turn visitor check-ins into decisions.

This demo shows how a museum can collect visitor data at the entrance and instantly turn it into attendance reports, visitor trends, exhibit performance, and marketing recommendations.

For demo purposes, data is saved in this browser using local storage. In a real implementation, it can flow into Excel, Airtable, Salesforce, HubSpot, Blackbaud, Tessitura, or another CRM.
